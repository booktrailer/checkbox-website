About the Creator

R

Randy, Developer & Productivity Enthusiast

Randy is a student developer and productivity enthusiast who built Schoology Checkbox to solve a simple but annoying problem: keeping track of assignments across endless Schoology pages. After noticing how many classmates struggled with cluttered to-do lists and unfinished tasks buried inside modules, he designed a lightweight extension that adds instant checkboxes, cleaner organization, and a calmer workflow. A believer in frictionless design, Randy focuses on creating tools that quietly automate the boring parts of school so students can focus on the work that actually matters. Schoology Checkbox is the first of several small utilities he hopes to release to help students reclaim time, reduce stress, and make digital learning simpler.
